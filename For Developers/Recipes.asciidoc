= Recipes

== Compile and/or install required software

Build/install the software in following order:

1. link:Recipes/cmake[CMake]
1. link:Recipes/gmp[GMP] (required by GCC)
1. link:Recipes/mpfr[MPFR] (required by GCC)
1. link:Recipes/mpc[MPC] (required by GCC
1. link:Recipes/gcc[GCC]
1. link:Recipes/openmpi[open-mpi]
1. link:Recipes/hdf5[HDF5]
1. link:Recipes/gsl[GSL]
1. link:Recipes/h5hut[H5hut]
1. link:Recipes/boost[Boost]
1. optional: link:Recipes/openblas[OpenBLAS] (OPAL with SAAMG solver)
1. optional: link:Recipes/parmetis[ParMETIS] (OPAL with SAAMG solver)
1. optional: link:Recipes/trilinos[Trilinos] (OPAL with SAAMG solver)
