= Recipes

== Compile and/or install required software

Build/install the software in following order:

. link:https://gitlab.psi.ch/OPAL/build-recipes/raw/master/build-gmp[GMP] (required for GCC)
. link:https://gitlab.psi.ch/OPAL/build-recipes/raw/master/build-mpfr[MPFR] (required for GCC)
. link:https://gitlab.psi.ch/OPAL/build-recipes/raw/master/build-mpc[MPC] (required for GCC)
. link:https://gitlab.psi.ch/OPAL/build-recipes/raw/master/build-gcc[GCC]
. link:https://gitlab.psi.ch/OPAL/build-recipes/raw/master/build-cmake[CMake]
. link:https://gitlab.psi.ch/OPAL/build-recipes/raw/master/build-open-mpi[open-mpi]
. link:https://gitlab.psi.ch/OPAL/build-recipes/raw/master/build-hdf5[HDF5]
. link:https://gitlab.psi.ch/OPAL/build-recipes/raw/master/build-gsl[GSL]
. link:https://gitlab.psi.ch/OPAL/build-recipes/raw/master/build-h5hut[H5hut]
. link:https://gitlab.psi.ch/OPAL/build-recipes/raw/master/build-boost[Boost]
. optional: link:https://gitlab.psi.ch/OPAL/build-recipes/raw/master/build-parmetis[ParMETIS] (OPAL with SAAMG solver)
. optional: link:https://gitlab.psi.ch/OPAL/build-recipes/raw/master/build-openblas[OpenBLAS] (OPAL with SAAMG solver)
. optional: link:https://gitlab.psi.ch/OPAL/build-recipes/raw/master/build-trilinos[Trilinos] (OPAL with SAAMG solver)
